How to have a Great Start to Your Day

Have you ever made a promise to yourself saying " I will be productive tomorrow" just to find that you feel unmotivated and tired after waking up? Morning is the most important time of the day, it sets the tone for how you see the rest of your day. Do you find that you spend more time lounging on your bed than doing work you should have done days ago? Here are some activities you can add to your morning routine to help you feel fresh and awake throughout the day.

Ideas to Consider:

Drinking a cup of water when you wake up is a great way to refresh yourself when you wake up and it is great for your health! by drinking a glass of water, you will feel more awake and energized. Drinking water in the morning can also help your skin look healthier and increase your motabolism.
Doing exercise when you wake up is a great way to get your heart pumping and ready to tackle all of your coming tasks. Doing exercise is also a great way to wake yourself up and relieve stress. This will also help with your circulation and is amazing for your health. Exercise doesn't need to be hard, deep breathing, walking, and stretching all count as exercise!
Reading a book is a great way to work your mind and get yourself thinking once you wake up. By grabbing a book when you wake up, it will truly motivate you to make the most of your day. It will help you grow with facts and inspiration, as well as give you comfort and excitement. The morning is also the time where your brain retains the most amount of information. reading is also a great way to relax your mind and get rid of stress.
Journaling or writing to-do lists when you wake up is a great way to plan and see the day ahead of you. This will motivate you to do everything that checks off your list, as well as tracking how productive you are everyday. Journaling can be a really fun activity that lets you go wild with creativity and also allows your to write stories about your day and feel organized with your emotions and your tasks. To-do lists will motivate you to do more tasks in the day as well as making you feel more in control; therefore, giving you less stress.
Have you found yourself spending the whole day on your bed watching TV or scrolling on your phone, unmotivated to do anything other than go on social media or sleep? Making your bed may just be the cure fo this problem. making your bed may seem like a tedious thing to do everyday; however, tidying your bed makes your room look more organized and may motivate you to do more work and will make you feel more productive right when you get up. Crawling into a made bed at night will also improve your quality of sleep.
Eating breakfast is a great way to energize your brain and your body. Squeezing in a quick breakfast in your busy day will make a great difference in how you feel, physically and mentally. Studies have also shown that eating something in the morning will improve your motabolism and also your emotions. Eating breakast will aslo improve your focus and allow your to make healthy food choices because you wont be as hungry when lunch rolls in.
Getting sunlight into your room or going out and basking in the sun is a great way to get motivated to enjoy the rest of your day. This is also a really nice and natural way to wake yourself up and connect with nature. It's also a very good source of vitamin D. Staying out in the sunlight for a few minutes will help to increase your mood and also takes stress away.
Another great way to feel refreshed in the morning is to put on your favourite music. This will help to increase your energy and mood, it also creates a fun and welcoming atmosphere. Listening to your favourite music helps you relax and feel happier doing your tasks. it also helps to set the mood for the rest of your day. Music is such a powerful aspect of life, make sure to use it to your advantage!
Lastly, dressing up and wearing clothes other than pyjamas will help to motivate you to not stay in bed and get things done. Dressing up is a quick and easy way to trick your brain into thinking in a more productive and organized way. Dressing nicely will also help you feel more awake and ready to tackle anything that comes your way. Dressing nicely will also make you feel great and confident in your work as well as with your attitude.
